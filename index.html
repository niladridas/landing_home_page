About Me

At Sandia National Laboratories I design algorithms and tools for Bayesian inference, ML, controls & optimal experiment design. I have used these tools in material science, climate science, power systems, robotics, & satellites.
I am passionate about teaching people how to apply these techniques in their own work and to further advance our understanding of these areas.
